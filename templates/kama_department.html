KAMA(Kenya Anglican Men's Association)

  • Rev John Kibicho
  • Jan 25, 2025

KAMA at St. Andrews Cathedral Thika is a fellowship of men dedicated to spiritual growth, service, and mutual support. We strive to strengthen our faith, build strong families, and contribute positively to our community. We believe in the power of men working together for the betterment of the church and society.

We engage in a variety of activities, including Bible studies, prayer meetings, retreats, and community outreach projects. KAMA provides a forum for men to discuss issues facing families and the community, and to find solutions grounded in Christian principles.

"Iron sharpens iron, and one man sharpens another." - Proverbs 27:17

We are committed to fostering a brotherhood where men can find encouragement, accountability, and support in their journey of faith. We actively participate in church services and leadership roles, promoting Christian values and responsible manhood. Our goal is to empower men to become strong spiritual leaders in their homes, their church, and their community.

Building Men of God

KAMA at St. Andrews is a place where men can connect with other men, share their experiences, and grow in their relationship with God. We believe in the importance of male mentorship and discipleship, and we strive to create opportunities for men to learn and grow together. We encourage all men to join us and experience the fellowship and support of KAMA. Through service, study, and prayer, we seek to live out our faith and make a positive impact on the world around us.

Join the KAMA Brotherhood

We meet regularly for fellowship, Bible study, and service projects. We also participate in diocesan and national KAMA events. We invite you to join us and discover the many ways you can contribute to the life of the church and the community. Whether you are a seasoned Christian or just beginning your faith journey, KAMA welcomes you.

Come and be part of a supportive and faith-filled community of men. We look forward to welcoming you!
