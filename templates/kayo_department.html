KAYO at St. Andrews Cathedral Thika is a vibrant community of young people seeking to grow in faith and service. We strive to empower youth through spiritual development, leadership training, and community outreach. We believe in nurturing talents and fostering a sense of belonging for all young Anglicans.

We engage in various activities, from Bible studies and prayer meetings to sports, music, and drama. KAYO provides a platform for young people to explore their faith, develop their gifts, and make a positive impact on the church and the wider community.

"Let no one despise you for your youth, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity." - 1 Timothy 4:12

We are committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment where young people can connect with God and with each other. We encourage active participation in church services and community projects, promoting Christian values and leadership. Our aim is to equip young people with the skills and knowledge they need to navigate the challenges of today and become responsible and faithful members of society.

Empowering Youth for Christ

KAYO at St. Andrews is more than just a youth group; it's a family. We celebrate our diversity and work together to build a strong foundation for the future of the church. We believe in the potential of young people and strive to empower them to become leaders in their communities. We welcome all young people to join us and experience the joy of fellowship and service. Through mentorship, guidance, and spiritual nourishment, we help young people discover their purpose and live fulfilling lives.

Join the KAYO Family

We meet regularly for fellowship, Bible study, and activities. We also participate in various diocesan and national KAYO events. We encourage you to get involved and discover the many opportunities available to young people at St. Andrews. Whether you are seeking spiritual growth, friendship, or a chance to make a difference, KAYO has something to offer you.

Come and be part of a dynamic and faith-filled community. We look forward to welcoming you!
