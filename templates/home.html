Our Story

St. Andrews Cathedral, Thika, is a prominent Anglican church serving the community of Thika and its surrounding areas. As part of the Anglican Church of Kenya, it plays a vital role in spiritual guidance, community outreach, and social development.

  • Regular Worship Services: Offering various services throughout the week, including Sunday services, catering to different preferences and age groups.
  • Community Engagement: Active participation in local initiatives, supporting the less fortunate, and promoting social justice.
  • Christian Education: Providing programs for spiritual growth, including Bible studies, Sunday school, and youth groups.

The church strives to be a welcoming and inclusive community, fostering fellowship and spiritual growth for all its members. It is a place where individuals and families can connect with God and with each other.
