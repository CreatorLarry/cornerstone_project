Welcome To ACK - Childern Ministry

  • Mr Mike Doe
  • Feb 12, 2025

The Children's Department at St. Andrews Cathedral Thika is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth and development of children in a safe and loving environment. We believe that children are a precious gift from God and we strive to provide them with a strong foundation in faith and Christian values.

We offer a variety of programs and activities designed to engage children of all ages, including Sunday School classes, children's church services, Bible stories, songs, games, and crafts. We create a fun and interactive learning experience where children can discover God's love and learn about the Bible in a way that is relevant to their lives.

"Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it." - Proverbs 22:6

We are committed to providing quality Christian education that equips children with the knowledge and understanding they need to grow in their faith. We create a nurturing atmosphere where children feel loved, accepted, and valued. We encourage children to participate in church services and other church activities, fostering a sense of belonging and community. Our goal is to help children develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ and to live out their faith in their daily lives.

Nurturing Young Hearts for Christ

The Children's Department at St. Andrews is a place where children can learn, grow, and have fun while exploring their faith. We believe in the importance of creating a positive and engaging learning environment where children can develop their God-given talents and abilities. We encourage all children to join us and experience the joy of learning about God's love. Through Bible stories, music, and activities, we seek to instill in children a love for God and a desire to serve others.

Join the Children's Ministry

We meet every Sunday for Sunday School and Children's Church. We also organize special events and activities throughout the year. We invite you to bring your children and let them experience the love and care of our dedicated teachers and volunteers. Whether your child is just beginning their faith journey or has been attending church for many years, we welcome them to be a part of our community.

Come and join us as we learn and grow together in the love of Jesus. We look forward to welcoming your children!
