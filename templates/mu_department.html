Welcome to Mothers' Union

  • Rev Mary Anna
  • Jan 30, 2025

The Mothers' Union at St. Andrews Cathedral Thika is a vibrant organization of women dedicated to supporting families, promoting Christian values, and serving the community. We are a diverse group of women united by our shared commitment to nurturing strong families and making a positive impact on the lives of others.

We engage in a wide range of activities, including prayer meetings, Bible studies, workshops on family life, and community outreach projects. The Mothers' Union provides a supportive network for women to connect with one another, share experiences, and grow in their faith.

"She opens her mouth with wisdom, and the teaching of kindness is on her tongue." - Proverbs 31:26

We are committed to upholding the sanctity of marriage and family life, promoting Christian education, and addressing the challenges facing women and children in our community. We actively participate in church services and support various initiatives aimed at improving the lives of families. Our aim is to empower women to become strong pillars of their families and agents of positive change in society.

Nurturing Families, Transforming Communities

The Mothers' Union at St. Andrews is a place where women can find friendship, support, and opportunities for personal growth. We believe in the power of women to make a difference, and we strive to equip our members with the skills and resources they need to serve their families and their community effectively. We encourage all women to join us and experience the fellowship and fulfillment of belonging to the Mothers' Union. Through prayer, service, and advocacy, we seek to live out our faith and make a lasting impact on the world around us.

Join the Mothers' Union Family

We meet regularly for fellowship, prayer, and activities. We also participate in diocesan and national Mothers' Union events. We invite you to join us and discover the many ways you can contribute to the well-being of families and the community. Whether you are a mother, grandmother, or simply a woman who cares about families, the Mothers' Union welcomes you.

Come and be part of a loving and supportive community of women. We look forward to welcoming you!
